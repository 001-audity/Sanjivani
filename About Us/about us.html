About Us

Sanjivani is a one-stop medical service web application. This provides several medical services including hospitals' central information, online pharmacy, health tips, doctors consultants, the remainder for vaccination, and medication. Our motto is to relieve the unnecessary troubles which consumes much time of the patients for the developing countries like Bangladesh.
